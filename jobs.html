Senior Web Developer

Summary:
We are seeking a highly skilled and experienced Senior Web Developer to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for leading and managing web development projects, from conceptualization to execution, while ensuring the highest level of quality and adherence to project timelines. The Senior Web Developer will collaborate with cross-functional teams, including designers, project managers, and stakeholders, to deliver innovative and user-friendly web solutions.

Responsibilities:

  1. Lead the development of web applications, websites, and other digital solutions from concept to launch.
  2. Architect and implement scalable and maintainable web applications using modern web technologies and best practices.
  3. Collaborate with designers to translate design mockups into responsive and interactive web interfaces.
  4. Write clean, efficient, and well-documented code following coding standards and industry best practices.
  5. Conduct code reviews and provide constructive feedback to team members to ensure code quality and maintainability
  6. Identify and address performance bottlenecks, security vulnerabilities, and other technical challenges.
  7. Stay up-to-date with the latest trends and advancements in web development and recommend innovative solutions to enhance the development process.
  8. Mentor junior developers and provide technical guidance and support as needed.
  9. Communicate project status, issues, and risks effectively to stakeholders and project managers.
  10. Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define project requirements, scope, and objectives.

Requirements:

  1. Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field.
  2. X+ years of experience in web development, with a focus on frontend and backend technologies.
  3. Proficiency in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and related frontend frameworks/libraries (e.g., React, Angular, Vue.js).
  4. Strong understanding of server-side programming languages (e.g., Node.js, Python, PHP, Ruby) and web frameworks (e.g., Express, Django, Laravel, Ruby on Rails).
  5. Experience with database systems such as MySQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, etc.
  6. Familiarity with version control systems (e.g., Git) and continuous integration/continuous deployment (CI/CD) pipelines.
  7. Experience with cloud platforms (e.g., AWS, Azure, Google Cloud) and containerization technologies (e.g., Docker, Kubernetes) is a plus.
  8. Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
  9. Strong communication and collaboration skills.
  10. Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment and manage multiple projects simultaneously.

Benefits:

  • Competitive salary
  • Health, dental, and vision insurance
  • Retirement savings plan
  • Paid time off and holidays
  • Flexible work hours
  • Professional development opportunities

Quality Assurance Engineer

Job Summary:

As a Quality Assurance (QA) Engineer in web development, you play a critical role in ensuring the quality, functionality, and performance of web applications. You are responsible for designing and executing test plans, identifying and documenting defects, and collaborating with cross-functional teams to deliver high-quality software solutions. Your attention to detail, analytical skills, and understanding of web technologies contribute to the overall success of web development projects.

Key Responsibilities:

  1. Test Planning and Strategy:
    • Develop comprehensive test plans, test cases, and test scenarios based on project requirements and specifications.
    • Define testing strategies, including functional testing, regression testing, performance testing, and compatibility testing across different browsers and devices.
  2. Test Execution and Reporting:
    • Execute test cases manually and/or through automated testing tools to verify software functionality and performance.
    • Document test results, track defects, and communicate findings to the development team using issue tracking systems like Jira or Bugzilla.
    • Generate test reports and metrics to provide insights into the quality and readiness of web applications for release.
  3. Collaboration and Communication:
    • Collaborate with developers, designers, and product managers to understand requirements and acceptance criteria for features and enhancements.
    • Participate in agile ceremonies such as sprint planning, daily stand-ups, and sprint reviews to provide input on testing efforts and project status.
    • Communicate effectively with team members to escalate critical issues, prioritize tasks, and coordinate resolution of defects.
  4. Continuous Improvement:
    • Identify opportunities to improve testing processes, methodologies, and tools to enhance efficiency and effectiveness.
    • Stay updated on industry best practices, emerging trends, and technologies related to web development and quality assurance.
    • Actively participate in knowledge sharing sessions and contribute to the growth and development of the QA team.

Qualifications and Skills:

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field.
  • Proven experience as a Quality Assurance Engineer in web development projects.
  • Strong understanding of web technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and web frameworks (e.g., React, Angular, Vue.js).
  • Experience with testing tools and frameworks such as Selenium WebDriver, Cypress, or Puppeteer for automated testing.
  • Familiarity with version control systems (e.g., Git), continuous integration/continuous deployment (CI/CD) pipelines, and issue tracking systems.
  • Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills, with a keen attention to detail.
  • Strong communication and collaboration skills, with the ability to work effectively in a cross-functional team environment.
  • Experience with Agile methodologies and practices, such as Scrum or Kanban.
